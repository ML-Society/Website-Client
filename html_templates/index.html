ICL Machine Learning Society

As Imperial College London's Machine Learning society, we are dedicated towards nurturing and supporting interest in artificial intelligence. To accomplish this, we are organizing startup projects, academic workshops and industrial events for everyone to explore.

How it started

Our launch event attracted more than three hundred undergraduates and postgraduates from a wide range of different research fileds at Imperial. Bringing in speakers from industry leading companies including Google DeepMind, London AI and many others. We are proud to have hosted one of the first such talks at Imperial College London.

What we do

We are based around three things. Our tutorials, where we teach people introductory and advanced machine learning techniques. The events we host, where we bring in industry leaders to talk about their work and perspectives on artificial intelligence. And the projects in which our members apply their enthusiasm for machine learning to solve real problems.
